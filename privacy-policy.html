App Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously and we want to keep the privacy policy short, clear, and simple, while outlining everything you need to know about the app and the usage it does of your personal data.

From now on, we refer to "VideoQR" as "the app".

  1. The app needs access to the camera to fulfill its main purpose (taking videos).
  2. The app needs access to the device storage in order to temporarily save the videos you take before sending them to our server.
    • The app only temporarily writes a file and then removes it immediately after sending the file to our server.
  3. We do not process any payments ourselves.
    • The purchase required to acquire the app is processed and handled by Google Inc.
    • We are not involved in the purchase process in any way, therefore, we do not, and we cannot, collect any information in the process.
  4. The app requests Internet permission.
    • The app needs to send the recorded videos to our server to process the information which is the app's main purpose.
    • The app also send basic phone information phone model for data analytics.
  5. You accept the terms of this privacy policy by using the app.
